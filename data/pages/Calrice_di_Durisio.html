Clarice di Durisio

Clarice di Durisio da Foggia (fl. 15th century), was an Italian physician and surgeon.

She was educated at the University of Salerno and belonged to the minority of female students of her time period. She specialized in the diseases of the eye and treated only female patients.[1]
