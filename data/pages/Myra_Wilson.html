Myra Wilson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Dr. Myra Wilson is a British computer scientist. She is a lecturer in computer science at Aberystwyth University.[1] Her research interests are in the broad area of robotics, and she also teaches in the field.

Media work[edit]

She was a judge on the BBC television robot combat programme Robot Wars.[2]
